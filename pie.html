Grandma's Lemon Meringue Pie lemon meringue pie

One 9-inch pie 30 Min - Prep time 10 Min - Cook time 40 Min - Total 8 Servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbspTable Spoon all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbspTable Spoon cornstarch
  • 1/4 tspTea Spoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 tbspTable Spoon butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites
  • 6 tbspTable Spoon white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  • Preheat Oven: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  • Lemon Filling: In a medium saucepan ...
    1. Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt.
    2. Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest.
    3. Cook over medium-high heat, stirring frequently, until mixture comes to a boil.
    4. Stir in butter.
    5. Place egg yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
    6. Whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture.
    7. Bring to a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick.
    8. Remove from heat.
    9. Pour filling into baked pastry shell.
  • Make Meringue: In a large glass or metal bowl ...
    1. Whip egg whites until foamy.
    2. Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
    3. Spread meringue over pie, sealing the edges at the crust.
  • Bake:Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes, or until meringue is golden brown.
